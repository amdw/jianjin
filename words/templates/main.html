About

Jianjin is an application designed to help you manage and review your Chinese vocabulary.

It is designed to allow you to:

  • Record new words you learn
  • Categorise them (e.g. by subject, difficulty or exam)
  • Record notes about each word (e.g. example sentences, comparison with other similar words)
  • Revise and test yourself
  • Keep track of which words you find most difficult so you can focus on those

The name "Jianjin" comes from a Chinese idiom: 循序渐进 (xún xù jiàn jìn) means to make steady progress step-by-step.
